About NOMA

The Network of Moroccans in America (NOMA) is a vibrant community organization dedicated to connecting Moroccan-Americans across the United States. Founded with a vision to preserve our rich cultural heritage while embracing our American identity, NOMA serves as a bridge between two worlds.

We are a diverse network of professionals, students, entrepreneurs, and families who share a common bond - our Moroccan roots and our commitment to building stronger communities in America.
